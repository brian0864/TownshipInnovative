About Our Company

With expertise spanning multiple industries, we deliver comprehensive solutions that drive progress and development.

Global EPC & IT Solutions Provider

TOWNSHIP INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS LIMITED is a Nigerian-based company specializing in Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) services alongside cutting-edge IT solutions. Our multidisciplinary approach enables us to deliver comprehensive projects across various sectors.

We undertake turnkey projects, engineering consultancy, and project management services, serving as main contractor, subcontractor, technical partner, or consultant for government agencies, private corporations, NGOs, and individuals within and outside Nigeria.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and timely delivery has established us as a trusted partner for infrastructure development and technological advancement.
